Neo4j Books

There are a number of books available, the most seminal book is Graph Databases by Ian S. Robinson, Jim Webber and Emil Eifrem.

  • Practical Neo4j by Greg Jordan
  • Learning Cypher by Onofrio Panzarino
  • Neo4j in Action by Jonas Partner
  • Neo4j : Des données et des graphes - Prise en main de Sylvain Roussy (fr)

NoSQL & Neo4j

  • NoSQL Distilled by Pramod J. Sadalage and Martin Fowler

Neo4j Graph Gists

Neo4j Graph Gists are simple text documents describing data-models and use-cases in prose and pictures but more importantly in interactive datasets (provided in Cypher) with live queries representing use-cases running on that data and visualizing the results in a variety of formats.

  • Neo4j GraphGist Portal
  • How do GraphGists work
